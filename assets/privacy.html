Privacy Policy for Oasis'98

Effective Date: [22-08-2024]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Oasis'98, a peer-to-peer (P2P) chat application that prioritizes your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Oasis'98 is designed to minimize the collection of personal data. However, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Peer ID: We use PeerJS to establish a direct connection between users. A unique Peer ID is generated for each user to facilitate communication.
  • Usage Data: we dont collect any info from the users other than peer id, dont monitor anything

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Connect Users: Your Peer ID is used to establish a direct connection with other users in the chat.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, because our application relies on direct P2P connections, we cannot guarantee the security of data transmitted between users. We recommend using the app over secure networks.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Since the chat is peer-to-peer, your communications are not stored on our servers.
